This is the kick off seminar for the second delivery of the [FAIR programme](https://rse.shef.ac.uk/training/fair4rs/) that the RSE team has created under the management of Tamora James.


<object data="/files/Talks/BSBR25.pdf" type='application/pdf'></object>

This talk is an introduction to the open science through the angle of software and the FAIR principle applied to research software. You can find it freely in [github](https://github.com/FAIR2-for-research-software/Better_software_for_better_research) and reuse it as you see fit.
